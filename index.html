About Us

petsworld.com is India's first and largest online pet store catering from year 2003 to dogs, cats, birds, fishes and all your small companions. Petsworld.com is a one stop platform for all the pets' needs. Our team continuously works towards bringing you the latest products and brands. We strive to provide you with a quick and hassle free shopping experience by offering you a wide range of pet products and services including vets, boarding, lodging, grooming as well as education for making your pet healthy for life long. We have attempted to create a platform with over 2500 quality products for you. We assure our customers of unmatched quality and timely service. The website has been designed to make your experience simple and convenient to buy your products in the least amount and high quality. We constantly thrive to bring convenience and having a transparent system to ship your supplies each month.
